# XVA Engine

Monte Carlo–based framework for counterparty exposure and valuation adjustment.

## Features
- Simulation of stochastic paths for underlying risk factors
- Exposure metrics: Expected Positive Exposure (EPE), Potential Future Exposure (PFE)
- Credit Valuation Adjustment (CVA) calculation

## Structure
- `src/` : Simulation and exposure logic
- `notebooks/` : Exposure and CVA demonstrations

## Objective
To model counterparty credit exposure and connect risk analytics to valuation adjustments.
